About SGBirdsFinder

This website can identify birds in Singapore based on their features.

How to Use

Identifying a Bird

  1. Head to the "Identify" page
  2. Enter at least 2 characteristics of the bird you would to identify
  3. Click "Submit"
  4. A bird or list of birds matching your characteristics will be shown
  5. You may want to edit your criteria if your bird is not found

Browsing the List of Birds

  1. Head to the "Browse" page
  2. You may search for a bird or scroll through the table of birds
{% if session.get("isAdmin")%}

Admin Functions

  • Add new birds under "Admin">"New Bird"
  • Edit/delete existing birds in "Browse"
  • Log out with "Admin">"Logout"
  • An overview of all these functions is shown in the dashboard ("Admin">"Dashboard")
